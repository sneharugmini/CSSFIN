HARLEY QUINN

My first venture into web design centered around creating a website dedicated to Harley Quinn, one of my favorite characters. Through this project, I delved into the technical intricacies of HTML and CSS. I meticulously structured and formatted the site, refining my skills in both coding and design. This experience not only deepened my appreciation for the intersection of technology and creativity but also ignited a passion for web development.

TOMB DEFENDER

My coding journey took an exciting turn when I crafted an interactive game titled "The Tomb Defender" using C#. A playful twist on the renowned "Tomb Raider," this game flipped the narrative by making Lara Croft the antagonist. In this immersive experience, players became natives defending their sacred land against her incursions. Through coding, I transformed an idea into reality, weaving storytelling and strategy to create an engaging gameplay.

FIRE ALARM SYSTEM

Through the fusion of technology and innovation, I engineered a functional fire alarm system using Arduino that transcends traditional boundaries. Designed with inclusivity in mind, this system boasts an LCD screen featuring warning symbols, rendering it accessible to the deaf community. As a testament to my commitment to safety and usability, I ensured that the alarm's sound adhered to regulations while remaining audibly robust.
